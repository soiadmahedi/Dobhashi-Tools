## 🌟 About the App
Welcome to the official release repository for **Dobhashi Tools**, a premium, cross-platform application designed to help you explore, learn, and preserve the historical Sylheti Nagri script. Whether you are a native speaker connecting with your roots, a researcher, or a language enthusiast, this app provides everything you need in a modern and highly responsive platform.

## ✨ Key Features
* **📖 Comprehensive Dictionary:** Search thousands of words between Bangla and Sylheti with accurate meanings and pronunciations.
* **🔄 Smart Translator:** Instantly translate texts from Bangla to Sylheti and vice versa.
* **🔤 Interactive Learning System:** Master the Sylheti Nagri alphabet in a fun and engaging way with our unique floating (Anti-Gravity) UI.
* **⌨️ Keyboard Setup Guide:** Step-by-step instructions to install and type in Sylheti Nagri on your device.
* **⚡ Fast, Offline & Ad-Free:** Fully functional without an internet connection and 100% free from distracting advertisements.
